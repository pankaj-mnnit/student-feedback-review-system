About

About This Project

This Feedback Review System was built as a college minor project to give students a simple way to share their thoughts about courses and teaching.

The platform allows students to submit anonymous feedback, while teachers and administrators can review useful insights to improve the learning experience.

No sign-up is needed for students. They can open the feedback form, select the subject and teacher, rate the experience, and submit their comments.

Your Opinion Matters

A small piece of honest feedback can help create a better learning experience for you and your classmates.
